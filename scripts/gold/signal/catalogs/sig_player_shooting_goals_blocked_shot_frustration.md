# sig_player_shooting_goals_blocked_shot_frustration

## Purpose

Flags players whose shot volume is repeatedly blocked (`>= 4` blocked attempts), capturing match-level
frustration patterns where finishing opportunities are neutralized before reaching goal.

## Tactical And Statistical Logic

- Trigger condition:
  - `triggered_player_blocked_shots >= 4`
- Trigger logic is computed from `silver.shot` (`is_blocked = 1`, excluding own goals) so blocked attempts
  are directly event-derived.
- Triggered player outcomes and exposure context are enriched from `silver.player_match_stat`.
- Bilateral team context is preserved with symmetric triggered-side/opponent metrics for shots, xG,
  blocked shots, possession, big chances, and opposition-box touches.
- Severity diagnostics include `blocked_shots_above_threshold`, `triggered_player_blocked_shot_ratio_pct`,
  and `player_share_of_team_blocked_shots_pct` for ranking intensity.
- Similarity gate note: closest active shooting-goals signals are
  `sig_player_shooting_goals_shot_volume_monster` and `sig_player_shooting_goals_wasteful_finisher`; this
  signal is distinct because it is defender-block outcome-driven (blocked attempts) rather than shot-count
  or xG-underconversion driven.
